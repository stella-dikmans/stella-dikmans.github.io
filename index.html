welcome to my performative habitat

stella dikmans

Hi, my name is Stella (she/her). I am a 25-year-old German-Dutch individual who grew up in Berlin. With a background in sociology and architectural design, I am passionate about understanding how social structures are embedded in an ever-changing global environment and how we can collectively react to ongoing environmental changes and climate catastrophes. Through my work, I aim to explore transformation processes and investigate possible future scenarios to imagine collective solutions to global challenges. I believe that exchange and discussion are key to opening up new possibilities for seeing and thinking, and promoting heterogeneous coexistences. I am committed to creating social spaces and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
