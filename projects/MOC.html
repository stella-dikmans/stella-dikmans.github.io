stella dikmans

the world is a museum to colonization, you just don’t see it. the MOC is here to help you notice, challenge colonial thought, and dream of decolonial futures.

we are...

as a collective, we put emphasis on playful and thought-provoking ways to “MOC” our current norms and standards. Our projects aim to make decolonial education accessible - often using comedy, satire, and radical softness/love to approach the topic.
